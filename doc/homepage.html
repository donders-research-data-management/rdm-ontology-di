Welcome to the Radboud Data Repository

The Radboud University is a university with a strong focus on research located in Nijmegen, the Netherlands. The Radboud University wants to contribute to a free and healthy world with equal opportunities for all and to make a significant impact on a regional and an international level. This is the key message of our strategy A Significant Impact.

The Radboud Data Repository is used by ${userCount} researchers to manage their research data, organized in ${collectionDacDraftCount} work-in-progress and ${collectionDacArchivedCount} finalized data acquisition collections, ${collectionRdcDraftCount} work-in-progress and ${collectionRdcArchivedCount} finalized research documentation collections and ${collectionDscDraftCount} work-in-progress and ${collectionDscPublishedCount} published data sharing collections. Everyone can request access to the published data sharing collections.

When working at the RU, please contact your data steward for instructions how to archive and/or publish your own research data.
